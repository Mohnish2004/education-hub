# Rosetta Workshop

> Pre-user, User, PyRosetta, Rosetta Scripts

Rosetta Workshop is a week-long, immersive experience, targeted to new Rosetta users seeking to become more proficient through Rosetta's most accessible interfaces. It has been offered online and in-person. <br></br>
