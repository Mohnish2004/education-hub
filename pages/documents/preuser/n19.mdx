# Foldit Teaching & Learning Resource Manual

> Pre-user, Foldit, Modeling

This guide or manual aims to help teachers and learners get started navigating Foldit versions: Foldit Education Mode, Foldit (the normal version), and Foldit Standalone. There are linked activities embedded in the guide that lead students through navigating current, citizen science COVID design puzzles.
