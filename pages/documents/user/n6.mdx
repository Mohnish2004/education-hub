# Design2Data


> Pre-user, Enzymes, Foldit, Modeling, Wet-lab

Design2Data D2D is centered around a course-based undergraduate research experience that with the underlying scientific goal to facilitate academic crowd-sourcing to rapidly address protein design questions that would normally take isolated labs decades to answer.
